What is Quantum Computing?

A cutting-edge kind of computation known as quantum computing uses the concepts of quantum physics to carry out computations in a way that is fundamentally different from traditional computing. Bits are the fundamental building block of traditional computers, which we use on a daily basis to store and process data. Binary logic is built on bits that can represent either a 0 or a 1.
